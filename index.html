Helsinki guide

What you actually need to know

Where

Messukeskus, Pasila

Helsinki Expo Centre, one stop from Central Station. Walkable to Holiday Inn Expo and Tripla.

When

9–10 May 2026

Recommend arriving Thursday for Friday warm-up. Stay through Sunday for sauna recovery.

Airport

HEL · 30 min by train

Ring Rail Line direct from Helsinki-Vantaa. Pasila is the station nearest the venue.

Recovery

Löyly · Allas · Kulttuurisauna

Three saunas worth pre-booking for your Sunday. We arrange the slot if you'd like.

Where should I stay relative to the venue?
Pasila / Tripla is most convenient — directly connected to Messukeskus by Pasila Station. Central Helsinki (Kruununhaka, Punavuori) is more characterful and only 10–15 minutes by tram. We pick based on whether you prioritise race-morning friction or post-race city time.
Do you charge a planning or booking fee?
No. Our hotel partners pay our commission, not you. You get the same nightly rate — sometimes a better one — plus VIP perks like late checkout, early arrival, or breakfast where available.
How fast do you respond?
Within 24 hours, usually within four. Most conversations happen on WhatsApp during European working hours. We send back a shortlist of two to three options — you pick, we book.
Can you handle group bookings for relay teams?
Yes — relay teams of four or larger groups of supporters are some of our favourite bookings. We coordinate room blocks, dinner reservations, and shared transfers in a single conversation.
What about a recovery sauna or a Tallinn day-trip?
Both are easy add-ons. Löyly slots are best pre-booked for race-Sunday afternoon. Tallinn is a 2-hour ferry — great for a Monday day-trip if you're staying through. We can shape the extension around your race schedule.
